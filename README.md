📊 Tableau Sales Dashboard | 2023

Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Dashboard** built using Tableau to analyze key business metrics such as 
Sales, Profit, and Performance Trends. 
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance across time, categories, and sub-categories,
enabling data-driven insights.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Objectives

Track overall **Sales and Profit performance (2023 vs Previous Year)
Identify top-performing and underperforming product categories
Analyze weekly sales and profit trends
Highlight key business insights for decision-making
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 📈 Sales & Profit Trends (Monthly)

* Visual comparison of **current year vs previous year**
* Identifies:

  * Peak months (e.g., strong performance in later months)
  * Drops and recovery patterns

---

### 📅 Weekly Trends Analysis

* Tracks **weekly fluctuations in sales and profit**
* Highlights:

  * Average sales (~$3,162K)
  * Average profit (~$537K)
* Shows performance above/below average using color indicators

---

### 🛍️ Sub-Category Performance

* Compares **Sales and Profit by Sub-Category**
* Key observations:

  * High-performing categories: Chairs, Phones
  * Loss-making categories identified (highlighted in orange)
* Helps identify areas needing improvement

---

### 🔍 Interactivity

* Year selection filter (2023)
* Dynamic visuals for deeper analysis
* KPI indicators (Above/Below average)

## 🚀 Key Insights

* Sales and profit show **significant growth compared to previous year**
* Profit growth (+43.7%) is higher than sales growth → improved efficiency
* Some sub-categories generate **high sales but low or negative profit**
* Weekly trends reveal **performance volatility and peak periods**
* Late-year months contribute more to revenue
